Plant Care FAQ

Everything you need to know about caring for your new green friend.

Most indoor plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. A good rule of thumb is to check the top inch of soil — if it's dry, it's time to water. Overwatering is the most common cause of houseplant issues.

Snake plants, pothos, ZZ plants, and peace lilies all thrive in low light conditions. These resilient species make perfect choices for offices and north-facing rooms.
